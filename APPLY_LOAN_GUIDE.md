## üìä How to Apply

### Step 1: Click "Apply for Loan"
On dashboard, select the "Apply for Loan" tab

### Step 2: Choose Loan Type
Click "Apply Now" on your chosen loan product

### Step 3: Fill Application
```
- Select Loan Amount (drag slider within min-max)
- Select Tenure (choose months)
- Enter Purpose (optional but helpful)
- Click "Submit Application"
```

### Step 4: Verify Information
- Check review ETA (when you'll hear back)
- See next steps (what to do next)
- Confirm KYC requirement (if needed)

### Step 5: Track Status
- Go to "My Loans" tab
- See application status timeline
- Track progress (submitted ‚Üí review ‚Üí decision ‚Üí active)

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I apply for multiple loans?**
A: Yes! After approval, you can apply for another loan

**Q: What if my income is below minimum?**
A: You can have a co-applicant (spouse, parent) to combine income

**Q: Can I increase my loan amount later?**
A: Yes, top-up facility available after 12 months of regular payments

**Q: What happens if I make payments late?**
A: Late fee (2-3% per month) charged, affects credit score

**Q: Can I close loan early?**
A: Yes, early closure allowed after 50% repayment (usually)

**Q: Is there processing fee?**
A: Yes, 1-5% depending on loan type (can be added to principal)
